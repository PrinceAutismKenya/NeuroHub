Nicholas the Prince Autism Kenya

Founder & Neurodivergent Youths Community Ability

Prince Autism Kenya, also known as Nicholas Ndungu Irungu, is a Kenyan advocate and activist for neurodivergent youth. He is the founder of Neurodivergent Youths Community Ability, an Instagram account dedicated to raising awareness about autism, dyslexia, and other neurodivergent conditions. He also founded Nichosville Dyslexia Shamba Village and serves as a Nicholas Dyslexic Advocate. Prince Autism Kenya is known for his advocacy work, representing neurodivergent youth and working with non-profit organizations and governments to increase awareness.
